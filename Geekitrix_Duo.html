Duo Developers

~ Break through rounds with a couple of switches. It takes two to tango! ~

Description

A pair of programmers will have to work fast to solve the code by switching every 15 minutes , try your luck!

Round - 1

SLOT 11:15 - 11:45 am

Prelims

1. As the event name suggests there should be two programmers to solve one code.

2. The coder should switch after 15 minutes of programming.

3. Five minutes will be given for compilation and execution.

Round - 2

SLOT 2:15 - 2:45 pm

Finals

1. Shorlisted teams from the prelims will be qualified for the final round.

2. The problem statements in the second round will be more competitive than the first round.
